Our Story

About Adarsh College

Established in 2021, Adarsh College has quickly grown into a trusted centre of higher education in Reoti, Ballia — currently serving 1,200 students across three years with a dedicated faculty and strong academic foundation.

2021 — Foundation
The Beginning

Founded with a vision to bring quality higher education to Hadiha Kala, Reoti. Opened with Faculty of Arts, affiliated to Jananayak Chandrashekhar University.

2022 — Growth
Expanding Enrolment

Student strength grew steadily. 7 core BA subjects introduced covering humanities and social sciences to meet the needs of Purvanchal students.

2024 — Today
1200 Students Strong

Currently 1,200 students enrolled across all three years with 420 first-year seats. A growing community of scholars from across Ballia district.

🎯

Our Mission

Admissions 2025–26

Applications are now open for the academic year 2025–26. Follow the steps below to secure your place at Adarsh College.

Admission Process

1

Register & Collect Form

Visit the college office or register online to receive your application form and unique ID.

2

Fill Application

Complete all sections — personal details, academic history, and subject preferences.

3

Submit Documents

Attach self-attested photocopies of all required documents listed alongside.

4

Pay Application Fee

Admission form is free of charge. No application fee required.

5

Merit List & Counselling

Check the merit list on the notice board. Attend counselling for subject allotment.

6

Enrolment

Pay tuition fee and submit original documents to complete your enrolment.
